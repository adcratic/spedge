SpringEdge's messaging rest APIs can be directly integrate into software, Web based applications and modules to meet many common business communication requirements, and customized solutions. According to TRAI regulations, messages sent by commercial entities are classified into Promotional and Transactional message. Messages sent through Transactional SMS gateway service will get delivered to do not disturb or NDNC registered customers also. Though the sms is not of promotional nature, sometimes it becomes necessary to send out an alert or service messages to users. While account sign-up, choose a 6 alphabet Sender ID to send transactional SMS from. It is best to choose sender name similar to your business name so customers can easily identify your business, Also it will help business to build a brand name. All messages will be sent using this Sender name from transactional sms gateway using Transactional SMS API and SMS Platform.

To run a 2-way messaging service, a number is required to receive the inbound message. We offers short codes and long numbers to receive real-time SMS responses immediately and efficiently. These ten digit numbers are accessible globally and not just restricted among networks in India. Long code is an apt solution to strike a two way communication with existing customers hence resolving their queries and even intimating them about any notification.
